Ahmed Fouad El-Watan

Senior Partner

Three decades leading the firm, Ahmed El-Watan has brought Fouad El-Watan- Consultants, Accountants, and Auditors to the foreground of the Professional Services industry. With hands-on experience in all aspects of the firm, El-Watan created a school of thought and a ripple of success, leaving behind no problem unresolved. El-Watan is accredited by the Egyptian Ministry of Finance, Financial Control Accountants, the Central Bank of Egypt, in addition to being a member of the Egyptian Tax Association, the Certified Public Accountants, and BKR International.
